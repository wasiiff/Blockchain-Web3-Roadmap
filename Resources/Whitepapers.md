## How to Read Whitepapers

1. **Read the abstract and conclusion first** — understand the big picture
2. **Read the introduction** — context and problem statement
3. **Skim the math sections** — you don't need to understand every formula
4. **Focus on the architecture/design sections** — most practical
5. **Read the related work section** — understand what came before
6. **Return to math sections** as you implement things

**Start with:** Bitcoin whitepaper → Ethereum whitepaper → Uniswap V2 whitepaper. These three will give you 80% of the conceptual foundation.
